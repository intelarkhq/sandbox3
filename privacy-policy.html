This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect personal data in connection with our project ("App"), which:

  • serves to

    • provide supplementary features in relation to such information; and
    • use user-provided data for documentation access management;
  • consists of
    • a Web App hosted on Github Pages (front-end);
    • a back-end Google Apps Script project, which may communicate with other Google services and make use of publicly available API endpoints;
    • a Discord bot responsible for supplementary features such as sending notifications or updates about certain user behavior, or accepting or gathering input or certain information from the Discord platform; and
    • the use of any other automation features obviously related to the project, such as associated ROBLOX OAuth 2.0 services;
  • features, among others
    • account verification through ROBLOX OAuth 2.0 services;
    • user submissions and their regulation, viewing, and management, serving the core purpose of the project;
    • Discord-based updates, notifications, logging, and reports about the state of managed information or certain user behavior, as well as Discord-based handling of user requests, input, or other behavior and relevant content; and
    • aautomated documentation or information access management for certain parts of the Group.

3. Data Collection

We collect the following personal data:

  • ROBLOX Account ID: Collected when you verify your ROBLOX account via the Web App using ROBLOX OAuth 2.0 services.
  • Discord Account Information: When you or others interact with the App through Discord, we may temporarily process available Discord account details or other information (e.g., user IDs, usernames, messages) to handle user requests, notifications, logging, or other related functionality. This data is not lastingly stored and is deleted immediately after its intended use.

We do not collect sensitive personal data (e.g., financial, health, biometric information).

Please remember that this policy covers only data collected through the App, not all data collected or used by the Group.

4. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect and process personal data for the following purposes:

  • Authorization & Access Control:
    • To verify user identity and ensure authorized access to the App.
    • To manage documentation access for certain parts of the Group based on collected registration data.
  • Moderation, Internal Enforcement & Logging:
    • To process and regulate user submissions related to community moderation and roleplay/entertainment-based enforcement.
    • To provide updates, notifications, and logs regarding certain user behavior through Discord.
  • Supplementary Features & Notifications:
    • To enable Discord-based handling of user requests, input, or behavior and relevant content.
    • To send necessary notifications or updates about the state or manipulation of managed information.
    • To maintain Web App session continuity, enabling actions without requiring repeated logins.

We do not use personal data for marketing, targeted advertising, or any other purpose unrelated to the App’s functionality and the group.

Private Scope Disclaimer: This is a private App designed solely to serve the interests of the Group. It is not a public service, and users who do not actively participate in the Group are limited from using it, as further established in the Terms of Service.

5. Legal Basis for Data Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Legitimate Interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR):
    • Ensuring authorized access to the App and maintaining session continuity.
    • Managing internal enforcement, community moderation, and related logging.
    • Handling user requests, reports, and behavior within the App.
  • Performance of a Contract (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR):
    • Providing account registration, verification, and access management features.
    • Allowing users to interact with the Web App as part of its core functionality.

6. Data Retention

  • Temporary Storage
    • User ROBLOX account ID and authorization details are stored in the App’s cache for session continuity and are deleted within 6 hours of session inactivity.
  • Discord & Other Processing
    • Publicly available Discord data (such as user IDs, usernames, messages) is processed in real-time and not permanently stored — it is deleted immediately after fulfilling its intended function.
  • Account Information
    • ROBLOX Account ID and its association with user behavior within the App may be retained for as long as needed to enable proper review or audit of such behavior.

We do not retain personal data beyond what is required for the App’s legitimate functionality.

7. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose your personal data to third parties outside the group of individuals. However:

  • The App may use your data in legitimate requests to other API endpoints, such as retrieving additional profile data (e.g. your ROBLOX username) from your account ID.
  • As established previously, the App works with user submitted content. Your data, such as your account ID, may inherently become part of such submitted content, even when not explicitly mentioned by yourself (e.g. you may be automatically listed as the author of a file). This content may be viewable by other third-party users of the App for which the App is not responsible.
  • We may disclose your data if required by law or to protect our rights.
